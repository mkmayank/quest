Presentation Layer :
Translation of data between a networking service and an application;
including character encoding, data compression and encryption/decryption

Internet Layer
Packet switching network depends upon a connectionless internetwork layer.
Its job is to allow hosts to insert packets into any network and have them to deliver independently to the destination.
At the destination side data packets may appear in a different order than they were sent.
It is the job of the higher layers to rearrange them
